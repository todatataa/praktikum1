Service Pricing

At White Orchid Events, we believe in clear and simple pricing. Our platform is designed to connect event organizers with clients seamlessly.

Our Commission Structure

For every event successfully organized through our platform, we take a **10% commission**. This commission allows us to continuously improve our services, maintain the platform, and provide robust support to both organizers and clients.

What's Included?

  • Access to a curated network of clients
  • Secure payment processing
  • Dedicated customer support
  • Marketing and visibility for your services
  • Tools for event management and communication

How It Works:

  1. Client books your service through White Orchid.
  2. You successfully execute the event.
  3. Upon completion, the payment is processed.
  4. We deduct 10% commission, and the rest is transferred to you.

Our goal is to foster successful collaborations and create unforgettable experiences. Your success is our success!
