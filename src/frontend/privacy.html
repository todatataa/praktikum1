Privacy & Trust

Privacy Policy

Your information matters to us. This page gives a simple overview of how White Orchid handles personal data on the platform.

What We Collect

We may collect basic account details such as your name, email address, and information you provide while browsing organizers, creating an account, or sending requests through the platform.

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to operate the platform, improve your experience, connect you with relevant organizers, provide support, and maintain account security.

Sharing Information

We only share information when it is necessary to deliver platform services, respond to your requests, or comply with legal requirements. We do not sell your personal information.

Your Choices

You can request updates or removal of your personal information by contacting our team. If you have questions about your privacy, we encourage you to reach out directly.

Contact About Privacy

If you would like more information about how your data is handled, contact us and we will be happy to help.
