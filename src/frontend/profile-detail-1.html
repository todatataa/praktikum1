About John Smith Events

John Smith Events is renowned for crafting unforgettable music experiences in Maribor and beyond. With years of expertise, John and his team specialize in orchestrating everything from intimate acoustic nights to large-scale outdoor festivals. Their passion for music combined with meticulous planning ensures every event hits the perfect note.

Whether you're looking to host a lively concert in a city square, a sophisticated classical recital, or a vibrant music festival, John Smith Events brings a unique blend of creativity and precision to each project. They handle all aspects, including venue selection, artist liaison, sound and lighting production, and audience engagement, delivering seamless and captivating events.
